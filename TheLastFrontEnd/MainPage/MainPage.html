      Seo Eun-hyun

      "But if regression is a fate from which I can never escape, I will accept it. And within this cycle, I will find my own answer. Just like how today, I embraced my connections and cast off my obsessions! After achieving Eternal Life, I will find an answer I can truly accept for myself! I am not Seo Eun-hyun, the Earthling from a civilized society. I am the Regressor (回歸者)" - Seo Eun-hyun.

      Seo Eunhyun is an Ender of the Supreme Deity rank, as well as the Immortal Beast King, called Star Genesis Supreme Deity walking on the path of the Dao of Myriad Stars. He is the Owner of Myriad Stars with the symbol of [Galaxy with Three Tails]. He is the first Ender ever to independently form a soul and step into the rank of Supreme Deity.

      He governs over the Qi Refining stages of 1st Star, 2nd Star and 7th Star, the Realms of Qi Building, Core Formation, Earth Axis, Star Shattering, Sacred Vessel, Vestige Liberation Immortal, Upper Immortal and Great Net Immortal. He also governs the divine power of the Entering Nirvana Realm.

      In the 2010th Cycle, he succeeded in scattering himself through the Domain of Purity, creating and becoming his own unique cultivation system, Paper Flower.

      Realms

      Realms refer to worlds/universes within the Three Thousand Great Thousand Worlds. Mortals live their entire lives within a single realm. Cultivators traverse through these realms as they gain power and authority.

      There are 3 types of realms that Cultivators travel through.

      The Mortal Realms

      Mortal Realms is the starting and the lowest realms. There are two types of mortal realms:

      Astral Realm

      The Astral Realm is a realm similar to a universe. The individual worlds are a sphere and you can ascend endlessly into space. The only known Astral Realm is an unknown realm where Eun-hyun fell to during the 15th Cycle. However, 80% of Ascenders from Astral Realms call their realm Earth. (They call it Round World, which is the same as Earth in Korean [地球])

      Decaying Corpse Realm

      The Decaying Corpse realms are made of the corpses of True Immortals at the Vestige Liberation and Upper Immortal realm. Decaying Corpse Realms are realms where the world is flat surrounded by a world barrier. The Head Realm, where the main characters started their journey, is one among uncountably many Decaying Corpse Realms and has a unique property where the sun and moon are like eyes. It is an ominous place that is dangerous to Enders and True Immortals, annihilating many gods in the past.

      The Middle Realms

      In the middle realms, due to the immense heaven and earth spiritual energy, babies become Qi Gathering cultivators from the moment of conception. They are born as cultivators. Some with a bit more talent are born already at the 1st or 2nd stage of Qi Refining.

      Middle Realms are the corpses of True Immortals who have reached the Great Net realm or above. Great Net Immortal's bodies are on themselves Middle Realms where living being can grow and cultivate.

      Moreover, the human race seems to be considered somewhat of a ruling class and enjoys quite a high status.

      Cultivation Realms

      Cultivation, or the Dao of Immortal Cultivation, is the process of elevating oneself from a mortal to an Immortal. As one progresses through the stages of Cultivation, they gain lifespan, regenerative abilities, and, for Heaven/Earth practitioners, come to resemble stars and eventually effect Fate itself. Cultivators themselves bring change to the Heavenly Mechanism, and since ancient times, cultivators have also been called Heaven-Defiers.

      The cultivation realms can be divided into the Three Great Boundaries Of Cultivation (수선삼계/修仙三界): Minor Boundary (소경계/小境界), Middle Boundary (중경계/中境界), and Great Boundary (대경계/大境界). At the Minor Boundary, one begins to break free of their mortality. At the Middle Boundary, one wields attraction forces. At the Great Boundary, one becomes a True Immortal. Star Shattering is the stage where one begins to step into divinity.

      Cultivation in the Three Thousand Worlds can be grouped into three categories: Heaven Path, Earth Path, and Heart Path. The first two are also known as the Immortal Family Methods which share the same cultivation stages found above. In contrast, the Heart Path has its own distinct realms. Certain martial arts/Heart Path realms have their equivalent among the Heaven/Earth Path realms; these comparisons are shown in the table above.

      Heaven Path

      The Heaven Path is practised by the Heaven Tribe, including the Human Race, whose enlightenments are based on [Heaven]. Their cultivation revolves around conducting rituals at the Plane of Fate to descend to the Planes of Qi/Soul.

      Earth Path

      The Earth Path is practiced by the Earth Tribe, commonly made up of animals who gain a spiritual awakening, demon beasts; whose enlightenments are based on [Earth]. Their cultivation revolves around elevating from the Plane of Qi to the Planes of Soul/Fate by exploding yin-yang spiritual energy within their bodies. Those who survive and withstand the explosions will strengthen themselves.

      Heart Path

      The Heart Path is practiced by the Heart Tribe. Those who obsessively struggle find themselves becoming a Heart Tribe member. Martial Arts and consciousness techniques mastered to the extreme may also ascend to the Heart Path. They masterfully manipulate the Plane of Soul, higher than Qi and lower than Fate.
